Dirty Dishes & Empty Plates

I'm Executive Chef Anthony Redmond. I enjoy teasing the senses to enrage the appetite, making his guest suffer a voracious need to devour the delicious dishes before them. On this site I will take the users on a journey meant to arouse each of the culinary senses: Aroma, taste, feel, until one can literally hear one's growling stomach demanding to be satisfied. Within also are tools to help those across the full spectrum of from home chef to professional, "What's a spatula" to "tempering eggs is indeed an art, but an attainable one" achieve dishes that will thrill them through every stage: prep, cooking, and eating; delighting their guests.
